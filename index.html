Our Love Story

We first crossed paths during a competitive game of trivia at the local tavern.

After some cheeky back and forth banter, numbers were exchanged and a first date took place the very next day with dinner and a movie. It was only 4 weeks later that Yosi packed her bags and moved in with Ery, and their whirlwind romance began.

5 years later, we’ve spent a 6-month stint in Queenstown, bought our very first home, and welcomed 2 mischievous kittens to our family. After travelling through New Zealand, Ery finally popped the question – and Yosi said yes!
